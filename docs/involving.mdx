# Get Involved

Getting involved into Orivon is not just contributing on a project, it's building the future of the Web3  

Becoming part of Orivon means having a chance to place you as one of the founding fathers who changed Web3 forever  
Means that you know Orivon leads to improve drastically the Web3 ecosystem and make it ready for the worldwide adoption  
And finally make Web3 a widespread technology

We can build big things togheter, and here I will explain how you can do it

## How I can get involved

It does not matter what your skills currently are, there are very valuable roles which you can take part into Orivon, once you get involved efforts will be paid out with a fair amount of governance tokens into the Orivon DAO once it will be finally launched

If you are interested into general contributions into deeper and more technical discussions you can look up at our forum [**OrivonStack**](https://orivonstack.com)

Then, your first question will probably be: "How I can actually help you?"
