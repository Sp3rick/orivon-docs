# Community channels

Orivon has multiple community channels which you connect with, join to the one represents best your needs

- [**Discord**](https://discord.gg/DuRg87MvgD) *Main community channel, roles recognition, getting involved, operational on Orivon growth and developing*

- [**OrivonStack**](https://orivonstack.com) *Our forum to discuss about Orivon design, documentation changes, solve technical challenges, proposing strategy improvements, setting new standards across Web3 related to Orivon and more*

- [**Docs Github**]() *contribute to this documentation and propose adds or changes*

- [**Telegram**](t.me/OrivonBrowser) *Telegram group for general support and discussions*
